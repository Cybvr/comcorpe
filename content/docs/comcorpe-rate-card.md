# Comcorpᵉ Africa Growth Pod Pricing

This pricing model is for English-speaking African markets, including Nigeria, South Africa, Kenya, and Ghana.

We don't sell hours. We sell outcomes:

> Diagnose -> Design -> Deploy

## Internal Africa Talent Tiers

| Tier | Talent type | Internal cost |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Tier 1 | Junior / execution support | $20-$35/hr |
| Tier 2 | Mid-level specialist | $35-$55/hr |
| Tier 3 | Senior specialist / pod lead | $55-$80/hr |

Use these rates to estimate delivery cost. Do not sell them as client-facing day rates.
